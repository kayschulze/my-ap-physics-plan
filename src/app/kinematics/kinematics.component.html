Kinematics Equation Finder Application

Kinematics is the study of objects at motion with constant acceleration. One early challenge of physics is determining which equation to use. There are four main kinematics equations. To find the correct equation, it is important to have a plan. That plan includes:

  1. Reading and/or understanding the problem. Drawing a diagram helps.
  2. Determining which quantities are known.
  3. Determining which quantity you are looking for. This is unknown.
  4. Using this information to find the correct equation.

This Kinematics Equation Generator allows you to determine which equation will be most helpful with your given problem. For these five variables, identify:

  • Three values that you know.
  • One value that is unknown - but you are trying to calculate.
  • One value that is not applicable - isn't involved in this problem.

Note: Don't underestimate the importance of the variable that is not applicable. You will learn to use this to determine which basic equation form to use.
